〖⌘frac⟪⁓numerator⁓；⁓denominator⁓⟫〗

* A shorthand notation for entering fractions: 〖⁓numerator⁓//⁓⁓denominator⁓〗  ％ added a fake begin annotation character here to trick the parser into getting out of the quick expression state. (Technically this is a minor parser bug that should be fixed at some point.)
